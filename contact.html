Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to create a custom chandelier?

Custom chandeliers typically take 4-6 weeks from design to completion, depending on complexity and materials availability.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship worldwide. International shipping rates and delivery times vary by location.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy on all standard products. Custom orders have different terms.

Do you provide installation services?

While we don't directly provide installation, we can recommend certified electricians in your area.
