About Lumi Craft Studio Gift LLC

Bringing Warmth, Elegance, and Artistic Beauty into Every Living Space

Welcome to Lumi Craft Studio Gift LLC

A lighting brand dedicated to bringing warmth, elegance, and artistic beauty into every living space.

Based in 1012 Marquez Place Ste. 106-B, Santa Fe, NM 87505, we are proud to offer decorative lighting products that combine style, function, and atmosphere.

At Lumi Craft Studio Gift LLC, we focus on LED Chandeliers designed to elevate homes, studios, and commercial interiors with refined lighting solutions. Our products are created to do more than simply brighten a room. They are made to enhance mood, add character, and become a statement piece in any space.

Inspired by craftsmanship and timeless design, we believe lighting plays an important role in shaping the feeling of a home. That is why we are committed to offering chandelier designs that reflect both beauty and practicality, helping customers create interiors that feel inviting, modern, and memorable.

Whether you are decorating a new home, upgrading your interior style, or searching for a meaningful gift, Lumi Craft Studio Gift LLC is here to bring light and inspiration to your space through thoughtfully selected LED Chandeliers.

Our Mission

To create exceptional handmade chandeliers that blend traditional craftsmanship with contemporary design, bringing beauty and light into homes while supporting sustainable artisan practices.

  • Preserve traditional crafting techniques
  • Create sustainable, long-lasting products
  • Support local artisans and communities
  • Deliver exceptional customer service
