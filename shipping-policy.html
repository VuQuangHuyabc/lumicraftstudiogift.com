Shipping Policy

Fast and reliable delivery for your handmade chandeliers

Last updated: March 2024

Order Processing Time

Orders are processed within 1-3 business days to ensure timely delivery for our customers. During peak seasons, processing times may be slightly extended. You will receive a confirmation email once your order has been processed and shipped.

Shipping Options Available

We offer several shipping options to meet your needs:

Standard Shipping

  • Delivery time: 5-7 business days
  • Cost: $25 for orders under $500
  • Free shipping on orders over $500
  • Tracking included

Expedited Shipping

  • Delivery time: 2-3 business days
  • Cost: $45 for orders under $500
  • Discounted expedited shipping on orders over $500
  • Priority tracking included

Express Shipping

  • Delivery time: 1-2 business days
  • Cost: $65 for orders under $500
  • Available for most locations
  • Express tracking included

International Shipping

We ship to most international destinations. International shipping rates and delivery times vary by location:

  • Canada: 7-10 business days, $35-$55
  • Europe: 10-14 business days, $45-$75
  • Asia Pacific: 14-21 business days, $55-$95
  • Other regions: Contact us for rates

Please note: International orders may be subject to customs duties and taxes, which are the responsibility of the recipient.

Transparent Shipping Policies

We provide clear shipping policies to ensure transparency and customer satisfaction with every order. All shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on your location and selected shipping method.

Special Handling for Large Chandeliers

Due to the size and delicate nature of our handmade chandeliers, some items require special handling:

  • Large chandeliers (over 30 inches) may require additional processing time
  • White glove delivery service available for select locations
  • Professional installation recommendations provided
  • Insurance included on all shipments

Tracking Your Order

Once your order ships, you will receive an email with tracking information. You can track your package using the provided tracking number on our website or directly with the carrier.

Shipping Restrictions

We currently do not ship to the following locations:

  • PO Boxes (for large chandeliers)
  • Some remote areas
  • International embargoed countries

Delivery Instructions

If you have specific delivery instructions, please include them in the order notes or contact us after placing your order. We will do our best to accommodate your requests, though they may affect shipping times.

Damaged or Lost Shipments

If your order arrives damaged or is lost in transit, please contact us immediately at contact@lumicraftstudiogift.com. We will work with the carrier to resolve the issue and ensure you receive your order in perfect condition.

Questions About Shipping

If you have any questions about our shipping policies or need assistance with your order, please don't hesitate to contact our customer service team at contact@lumicraftstudiogift.com.
